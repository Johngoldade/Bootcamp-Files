The Hopes of an Aspiring Software Engineer

Article by John Goldade

December 12, 2024

The Beginning of My Journey

We all have that moment in life where we decide we aren't happy with where we are, and a defining moment happens when we face these woes. For me, I was unsatisfied with my current career trajectory. A skillset that was taught to me by my father had made me very successful but I kept wanting more. To apply myself to learning another skill and "level up" my career was a motivating factor. My goal: software engineering and the hope of one day getting into Data Science and Machine learning. Signing up for bootcamp was step one.

KU Bootcamp

Signing up for a bootcamp with the University of Kansas was actually very easy. Following this link, it was very easy to sign up for the 24 week program. As I have only currently just started class, the experience is incomplete, but I can say that after a few classes, I already love doing this. I pray I will never regret this decision.
